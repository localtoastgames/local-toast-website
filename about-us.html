What we're making:

Our focus is currently on our latest endeavor; Project: Vantage!
Project: Vantage is intended to disrupt what we perceive to be an unfortunate state of affairs in the Virtual Tabletop space. All Virtual Tabletops applications that we see in the market right now seem hell-bent on carving out their own piece of the user-base and trapping them into an inflexible toolset that means that leaving the application for a competitor means throwing out all the work they spent and exclusive education they've built up using the app and starting all over with another app that will likely do the same thing to them.
Project: Vantage is our passion project for the Tabletop Gaming community, with a focus on 5th edition Dungeons & Dragons. It will aim to disrupt the current Virtual Tabletop space - seeking to open up the space and tooling so that Game Masters and players can more easily switch between applications; ultimately making life much better for all Game Masters and players. We aim to do this with unprecedented accessibility to our own Virtual Tabletop offering.

Project Focal Points

  • User-Delighting Project: Vantage focuses on creating a User Experience that delights; prioritizing User Experience over feature bloat.
  • Flexible Our philosophy for Project: Vantage is to accommodate for all kinds of Game Masters and Players (and combinations thereof!). Groups that all want internal character sheets and strict rule enforcement should be accommodated for, while groups with some players that want to play with pen and paper and others that want to use D&D Beyond character sheet app. Groups that like top-down maps and groups that like isometric maps. Groups that want to invest lots of time and effort into their campaign between sessions, and groups that improvise epic storylines together as they go.
  • Streaming-Friendly We want Project: Vantage to be the go-to tool for D&D Streamers. We think the low barrier to entry, pleasing User Experience, and flexibility of our application will be especially appealing to streamers and we see our tool as a way for many streamers to easily and effortlessly improve the production quality of their streams (and positively impact the D&D community!)
